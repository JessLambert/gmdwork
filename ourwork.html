Our Work


MISSION:
Goldstein Museum of Design uses the power
of design to foster improved quality of life for
individuals and communities.

IMPACT:
Through design, Goldstein Museum of Design inspires empathy for others and advances knowledge.

COMMITMENTS:
We are a public bridge, connecting the College of Design to the community, and an open window into the complex narrative of historic and contemporary design. We are eager to learn about the students and faculty we serve – their needs, interests, and expectations.
